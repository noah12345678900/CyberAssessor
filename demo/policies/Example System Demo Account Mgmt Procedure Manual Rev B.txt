Purpose
-------
Defines account lifecycle controls for the Example System Demo information system
(synthetic). Covers privileged and standard user account provisioning,
quarterly attestation, and offboarding for hosts in the example-system-demo.local
boundary. Revises Section 4.2 to require dual-reviewer sign-off on
privileged-account retention decisions.

Section 4.2 — Quarterly Privileged-Account Attestation (revised)
----------------------------------------------------------------
On the first business day of each calendar quarter, the ISSO produces an
inventory of all privileged accounts on example-system-demo-ws01 and example-system-demo-srv01
and routes it to BOTH the account owner of record AND the system owner
for written attestation. A "still required" disposition requires BOTH
signatures; either reviewer marking "remove" triggers same-day account
disablement. Attestations are filed in the Example System Demo SharePoint workspace
under /Sites/Example-System-Demo/Records/Attestations with the Rev B template.
